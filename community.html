Top Community Issues in Marikina

  1. Flood Control – Some areas near the river still experience flooding during heavy rain.
  2. Traffic – Increasing vehicles create congestion during rush hours.
  3. Waste Management – Proper garbage segregation needs improvement.
  4. Public Transportation – Residents want more efficient transport options.
  5. Youth Programs – More recreational and leadership programs are needed.
