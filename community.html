Top Community Issues in Marikina

  1. Traffic Congestion – Heavy traffic along major roads and narrow streets causes delays and affects residents’ productivity.
  2. Flooding and Drainage Problems – Seasonal flooding and poor drainage in certain areas continue to threaten homes and disrupt daily life.
  3. Waste Management – Proper garbage segregation needs improvement.
  4. Public Transportation Challenges – Limited routes, overcrowded jeepneys and buses, and inconsistent schedules make commuting difficult for residents./li>
  5. Youth Programs – More recreational and leadership programs are needed.
