Marikina in 2035

By the year 2035, Marikina City could become one of the most technologically advanced cities in the Philippines. Schools may use smart classrooms equipped with interactive boards and digital learning tools.

Transportation could improve through electric jeepneys, bike lanes, and smart traffic systems. These innovations will help reduce traffic congestion and pollution.

Marikina may also offer digital government services that allow residents to access permits, pay taxes, and request documents online. With continued development, the city could become a model for sustainable and smart urban living.
