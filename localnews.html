Marikina River Park Improvement Project

City government plans upgrades for the popular public park

The Marikina City government recently announced improvements for the Marikina River Park. The project includes better lighting, more benches, and improved bike paths.

Residents frequently visit the park for jogging, biking, and family activities. City officials hope the improvements will make the park safer and more enjoyable for everyone.

Students Join Marikina Shoe Festival

Celebrating the city's famous shoe-making industry

Students from several schools participated in activities during the Marikina Shoe Festival. The event celebrates the city's long history of shoe manufacturing.

Workshops, exhibits, and competitions helped students learn about the local industry. Many residents attended the festival to support local shoemakers.
