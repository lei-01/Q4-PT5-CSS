Protecting the Marikina River

The Marikina River is one of the most important natural resources in the city. Many residents visit the river park for recreation, exercise, and relaxation.

However, pollution and improper waste disposal continue to threaten the river. Trash thrown into canals and streets eventually flows into the river during rainfall.

The community must work together to protect the river. Residents should practice proper waste management and participate in clean-up programs. By taking action now, we can preserve the river for future generations.
